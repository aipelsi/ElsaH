Microsoft Azure Analysis: The Intersection of AI & Microsoft’s Cloud Business Evolution

Microsoft has fully incorporated advanced AI algorithms and applications in the products and services it offers at the present time. In this article we will review the impact of integrating AI in Microsoft cloud services business, Microsoft Azure, and how it’s affecting Microsoft business structure. Microsoft Azure is a cloud computing platform that provides a wide range of services, including infrastructure as a service (IaaS), platform as a service (PaaS), and software as a service (SaaS). (“What is Microsoft Azure? Everything You Need to Know! - KnowledgeHut”) Microsoft Azure went live in 2010 and it has come a long way since then. AI, on the other hand, refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, particularly through the development of computer systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence. Microsoft has been slowly integrating AI into its services over the past few years and has been and is one of the first movers in the AI/ML applications. Microsoft has been on the forefront of the AI revolution for the last few years and possibly dictate the future in the upcoming years.
